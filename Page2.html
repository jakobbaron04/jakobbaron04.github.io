How to Tennis!

Groundstrokes

  • Forehand and Backhand

    • Forehands and backhands are the most common type of shot in tennis. Forehand groundstroke is a groundstroke in which you use your dominant hand. Backhands are groundstrokes using your weak-side hand.Forehands and backhands also use topspin when hitting the ball.

  • Slice

    • Slices are shots using a different type of spin than groundstrokes. Instead of topspin, you slice under the ball to get backpin. Thiis allows for shorter shots with more movement.
